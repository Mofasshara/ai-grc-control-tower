# AI Governance, Risk & Compliance Control Tower (AI-GRC)

## Purpose
A regulator-first system to govern AI systems by producing audit-ready evidence for:
- Swiss banking (FINMA)
- Pharma & Life Sciences (GxP / CSV)

This platform does NOT optimize model performance.
It proves governance, traceability, and control.

## Regulatory Alignment
- FINMA risk management expectations
- GxP / CSV principles
- Human-in-the-loop AI oversight
- Evidence-based audit readiness

## Core Capabilities
- AI system registry
- Prompt & RAG change control
- AI incident & hallucination tracking
- Cryptographically verifiable audit evidence packs
- Immutable audit logging

## Explicit Non-Goals
- Model training
- Autonomous decision-making
- Production personal data
